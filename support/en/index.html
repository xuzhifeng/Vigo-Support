What Vigo Supports

Vigo is a lightweight wellness and vigor-tracking app for supported iPhone devices. Some features rely on Apple Health / HealthKit access, and some signals may require Apple Watch data.

Common Questions

Why does Vigo show no health data?
Make sure Health permissions are enabled for Vigo, your device supports HealthKit, and Apple Watch or related health recording has collected enough data.

Why are some insights or trends unavailable?
Some pages need enough recent health data to generate stable results. If data is missing, stale, or limited, Vigo may show lighter guidance or ask you to calibrate manually.

How do subscriptions work?
Premium features are provided through an App Store subscription. Pricing, trial eligibility, billing cycle, and renewal terms are shown at purchase. Subscriptions can be managed or canceled in Apple ID subscription settings.

How do I restore purchases?
Open the premium screen in Vigo and use the Restore Purchases option.

Is Vigo a medical app?
No. Vigo is a general wellness app and is not a medical device. It does not provide diagnosis or treatment advice.
